ARBROATH, ANGUS

Founded in 1178. The home of Arbroath Smokies, the Declaration of Independence and Arbroath F.C.

Welcome to Arbroath

Arbroath is a seaside town within the region of Angus in North East Scotland. It is home to a population of around 23,500 individuals, making it the largest town in Angus.

Arbroath is famous for its involvement in historical Scottish events, and today is most popular amongst tourists for its local hotspots, including the Arbroath Cliffs, the Harbour, the Arbroath Abbey and numerous caravan sites and nature trails located throughout the town.
